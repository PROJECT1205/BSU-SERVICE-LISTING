              SPORT OFFICE

Step into the Sport Office, your dedicated resource center for all things athletic-related on campus. As esteemed
members of our faculty, you play a crucial role in shaping the holistic development of our students, and we are here to
support you in fostering a vibrant and active campus community.

  • Equipment Access: As faculty members, you have privileged access to a wide range of sports equipment and facilities. Whether you're organizing a recreational activity, leading a fitness class, or coaching a team, our Sport Office is equipped to provide you with the tools and resources you need to promote physical activity and well-being among our students.
  • Facility Reservations: Need to reserve a gymnasium, playing field, or other athletic facility for a faculty event or activity? Our Sport Office staff can assist you in scheduling and coordinating facility reservations to ensure smooth and efficient access to our campus sports amenities.
  • Athletic Programming Support: Interested in incorporating athletic programming into your curriculum or extracurricular activities? Our knowledgeable staff members are available to provide guidance and support in developing and implementing athletic initiatives that align with your educational objectives and promote student engagement and participation.
  • Safety and Compliance: The safety and well-being of our faculty and students are of paramount importance. Our Sport Office is committed to upholding safety standards and ensuring compliance with relevant regulations and protocols to create a secure and inclusive environment for all participants.
