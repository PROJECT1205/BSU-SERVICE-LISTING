              GUIDANCE OFFICE

The Guidance Office is a vital resource on campus, dedicated to providing support, guidance, and accountability to
students as they navigate their academic journey. While our primary focus is on offering assistance and mentorship, we
also play a role in upholding the standards and values of our university community.

  • Supportive Guidance: Whether you're facing personal challenges, academic struggles, or simply seeking guidance on your educational path, our doors are always open to lend a listening ear and offer compassionate support. Our trained counselors are here to help you explore solutions, develop coping strategies, and navigate the complexities of university life.
  • Accountability and Discipline: As members of the university community, we all have a responsibility to uphold its values and standards of conduct. In instances where a student's behavior or actions may have fallen short of these expectations, the Guidance Office plays a role in addressing the situation with fairness and accountability. This may involve discussions, warnings, or disciplinary measures as necessary to maintain the integrity of our community and promote a safe and respectful environment for all.
  • Conflict Resolution: When conflicts arise between students, faculty, or staff members, the Guidance Office serves as a neutral mediator to facilitate communication, resolve misunderstandings, and seek amicable solutions. Our goal is to foster constructive dialogue and promote mutual understanding, ultimately fostering a harmonious and inclusive campus environment.
  • Education and Prevention: In addition to addressing issues as they arise, the Guidance Office is committed to proactive efforts aimed at education and prevention. Through workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns, we strive to promote positive behaviors, enhance interpersonal skills, and empower students to make informed decisions that contribute to their personal and academic success.
