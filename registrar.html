              REGISTRAR OFFICE

We are pleased to introduce the Registrar's Office, your dedicated resource for all matters related to academic
administration and student services. Conveniently located on campus, the Registrar's Office serves as your go-to
destination for resolving payment inquiries, adjusting your class schedule, and addressing any conflicts that may arise
during the registration process.

  • Payment Inquiries: Have questions about your tuition fees, payment deadlines, or financial aid options? Our friendly and knowledgeable staff members are here to provide assistance and guidance. Whether you need clarification on your billing statement or assistance with setting up a payment plan, we're committed to helping you navigate the financial aspects of your education.
  • Schedule Adjustments: Encountered a scheduling conflict or need to drop a subject from your course load? Visit the Registrar's Office to explore your options and make necessary adjustments to your schedule. Our team will work with you to find alternative class times, drop unwanted subjects, or explore course substitutions to ensure that your academic journey remains on track.
  • Conflict Resolution: Facing a scheduling conflict or administrative issue that requires resolution? Rest assured that our dedicated staff members are equipped to address any challenges that may arise during the registration process. Whether it's resolving course conflicts, addressing enrollment issues, or facilitating communication with academic departments, we're here to help you find solutions and overcome obstacles.
  • Student Support: Beyond addressing administrative concerns, the Registrar's Office is committed to providing comprehensive support to students throughout their academic journey. Whether you need guidance on academic policies, assistance with registration procedures, or referrals to campus resources, our team is here to support you every step of the way.
