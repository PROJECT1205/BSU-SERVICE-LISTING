              GED FACULTY

We are delighted to introduce GED Faculty, a dedicated space designed exclusively for our esteemed teaching Located at the heart of our campus, GED Faculty serves as a centralized hub where educators can access valuable
resources, collaborate with colleagues, and inquire about academic matters with ease and convenience.

  • Restricted Access: GED Faculty is accessible only to authorized teaching staff members. To gain entry, simply present your student ID number, choosing from numbers 1 to 4. Once verified, you'll have unrestricted access to the faculty and its resources.
  • Academic Inquiry Center: Need to inquire about grades, assessments, or other academic matters? Our knowledgeable staff members are here to assist you. Whether you have questions about grading policies, student progress, or curriculum updates, we're dedicated to providing prompt and helpful assistance to support your teaching endeavors.
  • Collaborative Workspace: Foster collaboration and exchange ideas with fellow educators in our comfortable and conducive workspace. Whether you're brainstorming lesson plans, sharing best practices, or discussing pedagogical strategies, GED Faculty offers a supportive environment for professional growth and development.
  • Resource Library: Access a wealth of educational resources, including teaching materials, research articles, and instructional tools, to enhance your classroom instruction and enrich the learning experience for your students. Our comprehensive resource library is continually updated to provide you with the latest and most relevant educational content.
