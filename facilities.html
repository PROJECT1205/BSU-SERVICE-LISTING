              Lian Cafeteria

Are you looking for a spotlessly clean and comfortable place to enjoy your meals on campus?
Look no further than our university cafeteria! Situated conveniently within the campus premises,
our cafeteria provides a welcoming environment for you to savor delicious meals and recharge during your busy day.

  • Impeccable Cleanliness: At Lian University Canteen, cleanliness is our top priority. We adhere to strict sanitation
    standards to ensure that every corner of our canteen is spotless. You can dine with confidence, knowing that your health
    and well-being are safeguarded.
  • Ample Seating: We understand the importance of having a comfortable space to enjoy your meals. That's why our canteen
    offers plenty of seating options, with clean tables and chairs available for you to relax and dine with friends.
  • A Space for Eating: While our canteen provides a welcoming atmosphere for students to enjoy their meals, we kindly remind everyone that the canteen area is designated for eating only. We encourage students to respect this regulation, allowing for a pleasant dining experience for all.
  • Convenient Location: Located within the university campus, our canteen is easily accessible to all students. Whether
    you're grabbing a quick bite between classes or enjoying a leisurely lunch break, our canteen is conveniently situated
    to meet your needs.
