              CAS BULDING

Step into the CAS Building, where the pursuit of knowledge meets the quest for justice.
Designed as a specialized hub for students enrolled in criminology and psychology programs, this building serves as a
beacon of learning and discovery in the realms of law, justice, and human behavior.

  • State-of-the-Art Courtroom Simulation: Immerse yourself in a realistic courtroom environment, meticulously designed to
    replicate the intricacies of legal proceedings. Whether you're studying criminal law, forensic psychology, or courtroom
    procedures, our simulated courtroom provides a dynamic setting for hands-on learning and skill development.
  • Interactive Learning Spaces: Engage in lively discussions, case analyses, and role-playing exercises within our interactive classrooms and seminar rooms. Our faculty members, comprised of seasoned legal experts and experienced psychologists, foster a collaborative learning environment where critical thinking and analytical skills are honed to perfection.
  • Experiential Learning Opportunities: Beyond the confines of traditional classroom instruction, the CAS Building offers a plethora of experiential learning opportunities to enhance your understanding of the law and human behavior. Participate in mock trials, forensic investigations, and community outreach programs that allow you to apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.
  • Holistic Approach to Education: Recognizing the interdisciplinary nature of criminology and psychology, our curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the factors influencing human behavior, crime, and justice. Explore the intersections between law and psychology, delving into topics such as criminal profiling, victimology, and rehabilitation strategies.
