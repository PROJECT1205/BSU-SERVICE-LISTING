              Open Court

Looking for a place to shoot hoops, practice your dribbling skills,
or engage in friendly pickup games? Look no further than our open basketball court, the ultimate destination for basketball enthusiasts of all skill levels!

  • Accessible to All: Our open court is available to students of all abilities, whether you're a seasoned player or just getting started with the game. Everyone is welcome to join in the fun and improve their basketball skills.
  • Casual Pickup Games: Gather your friends or join a pickup game with fellow students for some friendly competition. Open court time provides the perfect opportunity to showcase your talent, work on teamwork, and enjoy the thrill of basketball action.
  • Structured Activities: In addition to pickup games, our open court sessions may include structured activities led by our experienced P.E. instructors. From skill-building drills to mini-tournaments, there's always something exciting happening on the court.
  • Safe and Supportive Environment: We prioritize the safety and well-being of our students. Our open court sessions are supervised by trained staff members who ensure that all participants adhere to safety guidelines and exhibit good sportsmanship.
