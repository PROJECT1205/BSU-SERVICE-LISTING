FACILITIES STUDENTS AREA

A campus service listing website is an online platform that provides information about various services available on a
college or university campus. These resources include department buildings, dining areas, health clinics, and student
support services. This website includes a directory or list that makes it easy for students to locate and use
information about various campus services, such as directions to that facility, hours of operation, and any other
relevant information. According to Razaeean et al., (2012) as cited in a study by Deventer & Lues (2022), the perceived
usefulness, usability, and website innovation of a university website positively influences the students’ satisfaction
with the website. Therefore, in the context of creating a campus service listing website specifically tailored for
freshmen navigation, our research will prioritize the consideration of these factors. The website will be created with
the intention of making it as useful as possible for first-year students by providing a comprehensive listing of campus
resources relevant to their needs. A major focus will be usability, with features like user-friendly interface, clear
organization, and simple navigation designed to make it easier for the students to use. Additionally, a focus on website
innovation will be done to provide fresh features and capabilities that appeal to the first-year students and improve
their navigation of campus services.
