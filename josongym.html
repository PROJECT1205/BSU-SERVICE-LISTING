              Joson GYM

Are you ready to elevate your game and fitness levels to new heights?
Look no further than Joson Gym, the premier destination for basketball aficionados and physical education enthusiasts alike! Nestled in the heart of our campus,
Joson Gym offers a dynamic space where you can hone your basketball skills, engage in invigorating workouts, and
participate in special activities that will leave you energized and inspired.

  • State-of-the-Art Basketball Facilities: Step onto our pristine basketball courts and experience the thrill of the game like never before. Whether you're practicing your jump shot, perfecting your dribbling skills, or engaging in friendly pickup games with friends, our top-notch facilities provide the perfect backdrop for endless hours of basketball excitement.
  • Versatile Space for Physical Education: At Joson Gym, physical education isn't just about exercise—it's about exploration, discovery, and personal growth. Our expansive gymnasium serves as the ultimate playground for PE classes, offering students a diverse array of activities and exercises to enhance their fitness levels and overall well-being.
  • Dome-Shaped Architecture: Marvel at the unique dome-shaped architecture of Joson Gym, which not only adds a touch of elegance to the space but also provides excellent acoustics and ventilation for a comfortable and enjoyable experience. Whether you're shooting hoops on the court or participating in special events on the stage, you'll appreciate the thoughtfully designed layout of our gymnasium.
  • Multi-Purpose Stage for Special Activities: Unleash your creativity and showcase your talents on our multi-purpose stage, which serves as the focal point for a variety of special activities and events. From talent shows and dance performances to guest lectures and award ceremonies, our stage is equipped to accommodate all manner of memorable occasions.
